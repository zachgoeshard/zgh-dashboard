5-Week Progression

    


    
⚠ Not medical/training advice — a few honest notes. A 20 lb drop in 5 weeks (~4 lb/wk) is very aggressive; ~1.5–2 lb/wk (≈8–10 lb here) is more sustainable and protects the strength and energy you'll need on the trail. A hard deficit + heavy training + a 40 mi/60 lb trip is a big combined load — prioritize protein (~1 g/lb goal weight), sleep, hydration, and break in your boots + loaded pack before departure. Build vest weight gradually and ease off uphill intervals if knees/feet complain. Confirm any big changes with your provider, especially alongside your current protocol.
